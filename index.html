Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Introduction

Welcome to FileMytax, operated by Brisca. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

⚠️ Disclaimer

FileMytax is a private tax assistance service provided by Brisca (Pvt) Ltd. This app is NOT affiliated with, endorsed by, or representing any government entity including the Inland Revenue Department of Sri Lanka.

FileMytax assists users through the tax registration process as a private third-party service. For official government tax information, please visit the official Inland Revenue Department of Sri Lanka website: www.ird.gov.lk

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you register and use our app:

  • Full name (first and last name)
  • Email address
  • Phone number and WhatsApp number
  • National Identity Card (NIC) number and document image
  • Tax Identification Number (TIN) if applicable
  • Residential address
  • Preferred language and communication method
  • Referral code (if used)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Process your TIN registration application
  • Contact you via WhatsApp or email regarding your application
  • Send confirmation and status update emails
  • Manage referral rewards and discounts
  • Improve our services and app experience

How We Store Your Information

Your data is securely stored using Google Firebase (Firestore Database and Firebase Storage), which is protected by Google's security infrastructure. Uploaded NIC documents are stored in Firebase Storage and are only accessible by authorized Brisca team members.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • Authorized Brisca staff who process your tax applications
  • Government tax authorities as required for TIN registration
  • Service providers (Firebase, EmailJS) used to operate our app

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at Tax@brisca.lk

Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. If you request account deletion, we will remove your data within 30 days unless we are required to retain it by law.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.

Contact Us
